About the Personality Development Club

The Personality Development Club aims to enhance the overall personality of students by focusing on their communication skills, leadership qualities, and confidence. The club organizes workshops, group discussions, and interactive sessions to help students develop soft skills and prepare for professional challenges. It fosters self-improvement, positive attitude, and team collaboration, ensuring students grow both personally and professionally.
